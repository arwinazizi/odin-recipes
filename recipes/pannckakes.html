Pancakes

This is my own delicious pancakes recipe!

Ingredients!

  • 8dl of Milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 4dl of flour
  • 1ml of salt

Steps!

  1. Start by cracking two eggs into a bowl and whisk them a bit
  2. Now have 3 dl of flour into the bowl followed by 5 dl of milk and whisk it for a couple of minutes
  3. When you feel ready, crack the last egg followed by the last dl of flour and keep whisking the bowl for another 30 seconds
  4. Now have the last 3 dl of milk in the bowl followed by 1 ml of salt and whisk the bowl until every ingredient seem spread
  5. Now have some butter in a round frying pan and start pouring the batter in to the pan
